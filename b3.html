"Nutella" Overnight Oats

If you are a chocolate lover, this easy oatmeal recipe is for you. It's rich and chocolatey, but not overwhelmingly sweet. It tastes like an indulgent breakfast treat.

It's perfect with some fresh fruits, a drizzle of nut butter or a tiny sprinkle of sea salt, which can help to bring out the chocolate flavour just a little but more.

INGREDIENTS

½ cup (45 g) old-fashioned rolled oats

½ cup (125 mL) milk*

Tbsp (7 g) cocoa or cacao powder

1 Tbsp (7 g) ground flaxseed

1 Tbsp (15 mL) hazelnut butter or 'Nutella'

1 Tbsp (8 g) chopped dairy-free dark chocolate (optional)

Sprinkle of sea salt (optional)

Optional Toppings

Drizzle of nut butter

Sliced strawberries

Chocolate shavings

Directions

1. In a food processor, blend the dates until they are smooth, scraping down the sides as needed. Add the oatmeal, ground flaxseed, almond butter, and cinnamon and combine, scraping down the sides once or twice.

2. Transfer the mix to a parchment lined baking dish and press the base into the dish.

3. Top with the yogurt and spread out evenly.

4. Place into the freezer and let freeze for 1-2 hours, or until the bars are solid enough to cut.

5. When removed from the freezer, cut into squares or bars of desired size.

6. Let thaw for 3-5 minutes before eating. Enjoy!
